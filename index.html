About Us

The Krachi East Municipal Education Directorate, under the Ghana Education Service, supervises and supports all educational institutions within the municipality. Our mission is to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education for all.

Our Mission

To provide and promote efficient, equitable, and accessible education delivery in the Municipality through effective policy implementation, supervision, and collaboration.

Our Vision

To be a model municipal education directorate known for excellence in teaching, learning, and educational management.

Schools

We oversee over 100 public basic schools across various communities in Krachi East including Dambai, Asukawkaw, and Tokuroano.

  • Dambai R/C Basic
  • Asukawkaw M/A JHS
  • Tokuroano Presby Primary
  • Monkra D/A Basic
  • Gyato M/A KG
  • Borae Islamic School

Departments & Management Units

Our administrative structure includes departments responsible for supervision, planning, human resources, Girl Child Education, Inclusive Education, and School Health Education Programme (SHEP).
